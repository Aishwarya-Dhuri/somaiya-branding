    What to do

    Safety of students and employees is a high priority at every college. Most emergency incidents on campus are going to be identified by students, faculty, staff or visitors. If students, faculty, staff, or visitors are involved in or witness a life-threatening emergency, it is essential for them to brought it to the attention of the concerned department.

    The emergency warning may come to students, faculty and staff through campus mail, text messages, posted announcements or assemblies.Stay calm and don’t be panic. Please follow the provided Action Guide.
