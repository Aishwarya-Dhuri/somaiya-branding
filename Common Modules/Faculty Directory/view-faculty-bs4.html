Introduction

Dr. Ninad Dileep Mehendale, Currently working as an associate professor in the Electronics Department at K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering. Prior to that, He was working as a scientist with the Institute of Microstructure Technology at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, Germany. He has also worked as faculty in Vidyalankar Institute of Technology and D.J. Sanghvi College of engineering.
Dr. Ninad holds a Ph.D. Degree in Microfabrication and Image processing from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. He was Gold medalist during his M.Tech. at NMIMS University. He also holds a Bachelors degree in Electronics and Telecommunication and Diploma in Industrial Electronics. Dr. Ninad is the owner and Chief- Researcher at Ninad`s Research lab at Thane. He was part of 1802 projects in various domains ranging from computer programming to robotics.
