Exam Cell

KJSIDS is constituent college of Somaiya Vidyavihar University (SVU). Exam Cell at KJSIDS is responsible to execute examination of all programmes offered by the KJSIDS. It is part of the Center of Examination (CoE) of SVU.

The exam cell prepares and displays examination timetables, coordinates and executes in semester and semester examinations, preparation of result gazette and executes all relevant work in connection to examinations. The examinations are scheduled in line with the academic calendar of SVU.
