Exam Cell

KJSCE is constituent college of Somaiya Vidyavihar University (SVU). Exam Cell at KJSCE is responsible to execute examination of all programmes offered by the KJSCE. It is part of the Center of Examination (CoE) of SVU.

The exam cell prepares and displays examination timetables, coordinates and executes In- Semester and End Semester Examinations, preparation of result gazette and executes all relevant work in connection to examinations. The examinations are scheduled in line with the academic calendar of SVU.
