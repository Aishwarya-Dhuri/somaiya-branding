Is SOMAIYA VIDYAVIHAR University a Private University?

Yes, SOMAIYA VIDYAVIHAR University is a Private University established under ‘Somaiya Vidyavihar University, Mumbai Act – 2019’.

Is SOMAIYA VIDYAVIHAR University recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC)?

Yes. We are listed on the UGC website.

What are the various constituent colleges of SOMAIYA VIDYAVIHAR University?
SOMAIYA VIDYAVIHAR University has 7 constituent colleges, offering various streams of study. They are:
  • K J Somaiya College of Engineering: UG and PG programs in Engineering.
  • K J Somaiya Institute of Management:PG programs in Management.
  • S K Somaiya College:UG and PG programs in Commerce, Business, I.T., Science, Media, Arts & Humanities streams.
  • K J Somaiya College of Education: UG and PG programs in Education stream.
  • Somaiya Sports Academy: UG program in Sports.
  • Riidl Academy:UG program in Design.
  • K J Somaiya Institute of Dharma Studies: UG and PG programs in Religion & Culture.

What is “digital university enrollment number”, is it compulsory to have before filling up somaiya pre-admission form?
Digital University Enrollment Number is the number which gets generated after filling University of Mumbai’s form. It is highly recommended to fill the University’s form first. However in case of technical problems with the University website or so, students can fill the somaiya’s pre-admission form first, and keep trying to fill up Universty form. Kindly note that at the time of Admission, duly filled in University form needs to be submitted.
