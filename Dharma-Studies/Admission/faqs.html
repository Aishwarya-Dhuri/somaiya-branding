What is duration of the BA program?
The programme extends over 6 semesters (3 years) and is a full-time programme.
When can I apply for the BA programme ?
The admissions process starts in May-June each year
What are the career prospects after the BA programme ?
This programme enables the students to pursue the Masters’ Degree in Yoga
Where can I get the syllabus for the bachelor's programme ?
Syllabi are available for download on the respective programme pages, which are linked from the overview available on the admissions pagehttps://kjsids.somaiya.edu/en/admission/bachelors
Are scholarships available ?
Yes there are need and merit based scholarships available.
Is there a hostel facility available ?
Yes, hostel facility is available and the application should be made along with admission.
Are there seats for NRIs?
Yes, there are seats for NRIs
What is the duration of the MA programme?
The programme extends over 4 semesters (2 years) and is a full-time programme
When can I apply for the MA programme?
The admissions process starts in May-June each year
What are the career prospects after the MA programme?
Our programmes are geared to train students for research and qualify as recognised teachers anywhere in the country. The MA China Studies programme specifically trains students for policy research, with opportunities in think tanks, and also in translation and entrepreneurship.
What if I do not have the Bachelor’s degree in the same field ?
The eligibility criteria are clearly spelt out. In some instances, there are qualifying Diplomas a student can take before applying for the Master’s programme; in other cases there is an entrance test that becomes applicable.
