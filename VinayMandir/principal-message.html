Principal's Message

I extend you a warm welcome to the beautiful Somaiya Vidyavihar campus. The Somaiya Vidyavihar Campus in the heart of Mumbai exudes academic excellence and has the most conducive ambience for one’s academic and other holistic pursuits.
We at S.K. Somaiya Vinay Mandir Junior College (SKSVM) take pride that we are imparting education to approximately 3500 students and is one of the largest Junior College in Maharashtra.

Our founder Padmabhushan (Late) Shri Karamshi Jethabhai Somaiya, the great visionary, firmly believed that modern education should be value based and strong on ethics. Dr. Shantilal Somaiya carried that legacy forward and the legacy in now being nurtured under the dynamic and visionary leadership of Shri Samirbhai Somaiya who is implementing the best practices from universities across the world.

On 26th August,2019 Somaiya Vidyavihar University became the first private university in Mumbai. It’s a matter of great pride and honour for all of us working on this campus. Our web-site www.somaiya.edu connects students from across the world, to our institute. Somaiya Vidyavihar Campus is a host to thirty-five institutions offering Certificate, Diploma and Degree courses at Undergraduate, Post Graduate and Doctoral levels in the streams of Arts, Sciences, Management, Engineering, Medicine, Information- Technology, Mass Media, Philosophies and Social Sciences.

We are committed to provide quality education to each student who enters the hallowed portals of the Somaiya Vidyavihar Campus and specifically SKSVM. Academics is an important aspect. We at SKSVM ensure that the students receive an environment which enhances the students’ personality along with academics and help realise her or his potential and becomes an important element in nation building.

Our students have won national and international laurels in the field of sports. At SKSVM we have been blessed with an experienced and dedicated faculty who take great pride and joy in teaching students and being their mentors in more ways than one. The importance of one’s mother tongue can never be overemphasised and the role it plays in a child’s education. We at SKSVM understand the importance of a child learning in one’s mother tongue. Somaiya’s have encouraged and nurtured this method of learning and our Gujarati Medium Secondary School bears testimony to this fact.

The methods of how knowledge is imparted and received are evolving rapidly in these times of a digitally connected world. We at SKSVM encourage teachers and students to use digital media and web based methods to communicate and learn as and when required. Seminars and workshops are organised for teachers on the campus to keep our teachers abreast of the changing scenarios in the field of education.

I once again welcome students to this prestigious campus and partake of the opportunities and facilities on offer. I can assure you, you will pass out of the campus an enriched person for all the experiences our lush and beautiful campus has to offer.

Dr. Shubha Pandit

Principal

S K Somaiya Vinay Mandir Junior College
