Goverance Structure

A Junior College is an educational institution that provides two years of academic education beyond secondary school. It is intended to prepare students for additional learning with more advanced speculative measurable. Students have to apply to junior colleges to complete their 11th and 12th grades after passing S.S.C. Board or any other equivalent board examination. Students typically attend junior colleges for 1-2 years.

S.K.Somaiya Vinay Mandir Junior College is one of the renowned College in Mumbai established in 1959 , with the noble idea of creating a new generation of cultured and value based human beings . The precious goal of this holistic educational institution is the intelligent , physical, emotional and artistic development of the learner. It is a Gujarati Linguistic Minority College, governed by the Board of Education, Government of Maharashtra.

For academic directive & regulation, the Institute is administered as per the guidelines Board of Education, Government of Maharashtra .

The Governing Body is the absolute decision making body of the Institute and takes major decisions of strategic significance and overall approval of the financial plans . It also directs the substantial achievement of assured educational aims and goals.

The college conducts all its administrative and academic matters under the inclusive guidelines of Principal, Vice Principal & Supervisors. While the emphasis on educational purpose is important the college have pioneered in offering various endeavours which includes Incentive Coaching Batch , Olympiad etc. ,it equally focus on the acquisition by each pupil of physical, social, intellectual and moral qualities and skills.
