Undergraduate (also named as Bachelor or Major) Programme is 130 to 160 credits programme spread over three years/four year in specific discipline which student can accomplish. Bachelors Programme offered in Somaiya Vidyavihar University are Ordinary and Honours.

Bachelor Ordinary programme are basically 132 credits programmes in discipline of Commerce, Business Studies , Science & Technology, Arts, Humanities and Social Sciences.

An honours degree typically refers to a higher level of academic achievement at an undergraduate level. Bachelor - Honours is awarded for enhanced 16 credits to 20 credits completed over and above Bachelor- ordinary programme of 132 credits in same programme.

Undergraduate / Bachelor / Major programmes in S K Somaiya College develops advanced theoretical and research skills, provides feed to learning curiosity of your mind, develops your entrepreneur skills, ensures academic achievement, ensures better experiential learning. giving edge to students to embark in competitive corporate market.

They will help you to become more competitive in the job market, which could lead to a quicker career trajectory in an industry you’re passionate about.

Enrich your learning journey with ‘Minor’

Minor Programme is 20 credit programme spread over three years in specific discipline which student can accomplish along with Major Bachelor’s degree programme.

Minor programme would allow a student to obtain a concentration in an area other than his/her major discipline enabling them to obtain enhanced degree in multi-discipline area and providing edge in competitive world . It will help you to become more competitive in the job market, which could lead to a quicker career trajectory in an industry you’re passionate about.

Who can apply?

First year Bachelor degree students of Somaiya Vidyavihar University are eligible to opt for a minor in addition to the (major) degree program(s) they are enrolled in.

Requirements

  • A student will be required to complete at least 4/5 courses (total - 20 credits) for a minor but specific minor may require morecourses / units.
  • Minors must be completed simultaneously with a major degree program.
  • A minor may not be completed by itself, independent of a major program.
  • Student may choose an intradepartmental /interdepartmental /interfaculty minor.

Process of Admission (for academic year 2020-21)

Step1

Step 1

A student must declare his/her option to pursue a minor program before the end of the first year of his/her enrolment.

Step2

Step 2

Students interested in a minor program should file an application form (available in admissions office) with the Admissions Office before 30th November 2020.

Step3

Step 3

Admissions Office will announce the list of students who are admitted in minor program by 15th December 2020.

On Completion of a Minor

A minor degree will be awarded at the same time a bachelor’s degree is awarded. Minors will be posted on your transcript and will receive a separate minor certificate for each minor program completed.

Why pursue a minor in Somaiya Vidyavihar University ?

  • You’ll have the opportunity to tap into resources offered, as you learn from esteemed faculty and practitioners .
  • Students interested in a minor program should file an application form (available in admissions office) with the Admissions Office before 30th November 2020.

Experiential Learning through Certificate courses

Certificate course is designed to give the students 2 credit points. Each certificate course is of 30 hours duration.

Certificate courses focus on trending domains that could introduce experiential learning. It will help students to delve into interests that have fascinated them.

Who can apply?

Any student of Somaiya Vidyavihar University is eligible to opt for any of these certificate courses..

Requirements

  • No prerequisites required for any of the certificate courses
  • Certificate courses will require a minimum number of 30 students to start with.

Process of Admission (for academic year 2020-21)

Student interested in a certificate course should fill an enrolment form as per the schedule announced on notice board or website.

On Completion of a Certificate Course

A separate certificate will be awarded on completion of each Certificate course completed.

Why pursue a Certificate course in Somaiya Vidyavihar University ?

  • By pursuing a Certificate course from the Somaiya Vidyavihar University, you get fast-track options to enhance your portfolio /resume with amazing interdisciplinary learning.
