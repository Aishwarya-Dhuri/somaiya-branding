Become a Multi-faceted Future Ready Engineer

An undergraduate engineering degree programme B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) in Somaiya Vidyavihar University  ,is a 4 years full time programme in a specific branch of engineering (i.e. Major degree). Students are required to earn approximately 160 credits spread over these 4 years in any one of the following Major programmes

  • Computer Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Mechanical Engineering

These undergraduate B.Tech programmes are designed to acquire knowledge and skills, provide a base for lifelong learning and develop an attitude for adapting to technological advances. The curriculum is a blend of courses in basic and engineering sciences, branch specific courses and a wide variety of electives ranging from Technical / Humanities and Social Sciences / Foreign Languages / Management. Internships in Industry / Academic & Research Institutions are also encouraged.

Academic Flexibility

  • Students enrolled in any of the above major programmes offered by the college can earn additional credits to acquire
    • Honours: if in the same branch as Major so as to deep dive in the subject
    • Minor: if in some other branch of the college or in other fields offered by colleges on campus so as to broaden their horizons
    • Dual degree: 5 year dual degree program (Tech + Management): in collaboration with K J Somaiya Institute of Management so as to accelerate their career path
  • Avail semester long internship in industry or research organizations

Who can apply?

  • An Indian student who has passed 10+2 examination in Science with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
  • NRI / Foreign Nationals / Children of Gulf workers/ OCI students
  • For detailed information Click Here

Requirements

  • A student who has Passed HSC or its equivalent examination with 50% marks in requisite subjects
  • Non zero score in Entrance exams such as MHT-CET/JEE-Mains (Paper-1) /Somaiya Entrance Test- Engineering (SET-E) / SAT (Scholastic Assessment Test) Exam
  • For detailed information Click Here

Acquire Interdisciplinary Skills with ‘Minor’

Along with enrolment in Major degree programme, a student can enhance interdisciplinary skills by choosing to enrol for a Minor Degree in another discipline and acquire additional 20 credits spread over 3 years. Minor programmes create opportunities to focus on multidisciplinary, real world problems as well as enable the students to widen their horizons which in turn help for better career prospects as well as higher studies.

Who can apply?

Students, with Major in some engineering programme

  • after completion of first year of engineering in SVU for enrolment in Minor in another engineering program.
  • after completion of first semester of engineering in SVU for enrolment in Minor in other than engineering discipline.

Requirements

Selection of a student, who has fulfilled the criteria for application to enrol for a Minor, will be based on

  • Eligibility specified by the Board of Studies of the Major of the student.
  • Academic background
  • Availability of seats in Minor Programme

Process of Admission

Admission will be on a case-to-case basis. Orientation and counselling of students will be conducted by the college at the end of first semester to enable them to make a choice of Minor programmes which suits their career path.

Why pursue a Minor Programme in Somaiya Vidyavihar University ?

  • Opportunity to acquire understanding in an upcoming field which supports the Major discipline and is useful in business or job profiles
  • Crafting a career path with a distinct combination of skillset and knowledge base.

Become a future ready Professional

A postgraduate engineering degree programme M. Tech. (Masters of Technology) in Somaiya Vidyavihar University, is a 2 years full time programme in a specific branch of engineering (i.e. Major degree). Students are required to acquire approximately 70 credits spread over these 2 years.

The college offers M. Tech in the following disciplines:

  • Computer Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering
  • Information Security
  • CAD/CAM & Robotics
  • Energy Engineering

Postgraduate programmes in K J Somaiya College of Engineering aim to develop advanced technical and research skills, provide feed to learning curiosity of mind and nurture professional skills. This will give an edge to students to embark in a competitive corporate world.

Academic Flexibility

Teaching Learning in college is enriched by features like:

  • Wide range of electives in areas of specializations and Interdisciplinary electives.
  • Opportunities for Industrial Internship and project work in Industry/ research Institutes.
  • Exposure to courses on Research Methodology and Professional practices.

Students having Bachelor Degree in the relevant field of Engineering and Technology

Deep dive in your discipline with Honours

Along with Major Bachelor’s degree programmes students can deep dive into an area of specialization with an Honours Degree. A student needs to earn additional 20 credits spread over 3 years in the chosen discipline. The Honours programme creates an opportunity to acquire deeper and advanced knowledge and skills in an upcoming field in Major discipline, which helps for higher studies or better job prospects.

Who can apply?

Student enrolled in B Tech Major programme and completed first year under SVU

Requirements

A student who has passed the FY B Tech examination may be permitted to enrol for a Honours programme based on

  • Academic background
  • Availability of seats

Process of Admission

Since the programme starts in second year, orientation of students will be conducted in the college at the end of first semester / year and interested students will be guided for choice and enrolment to the programme. Admission will be on a case-to-case basis which suits the student’s interests and career path.

Why pursue a Honours Programme in Somaiya Vidyavihar University ?

  • Opportunity to acquire deeper and advanced knowledge and skills in an upcoming field of Major discipline
  • Possible benefits of these credentials while entering a PG programmes
  • Opportunity to specialize in a stream at graduate level

Discover New Avenues

The focus of the research centres in the K J Somaiya College of Engineering is in-depth knowledge of concerned areas, exploration of a variety of tools, experimentation and detailed analysis of the results which leads to multidisciplinary solutions of real world problems. Primarily research is engaged in three major areas; Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Computer Engineering by researchers who are currently registered with University of Mumbai.

These centres, with additional research supervisors will now explore and progress in further more avenues.

Who can apply?

Candidates holding a Masters degree in relevant engineering, can enrol in Doctoral Programme.

Requirements

Selection of a student, who has fulfilled the criteria for application to enrol for a Doctoral Programme, will be based on:

  • Entrance examination by Somaiya Vidyavihar University
  • Academic background
  • Availability of seats in Doctoral Programme

Process of Admission

  • Entrance examination + Personal Interview of shortlisted candidates
  • Admission will be on a case-to-case basis,depending on research interest of the candidate and availability of vacancies with the suitable research supervisors

Why pursue a Doctoral Programme in Somaiya Vidyavihar University ?

  • Wide scope of multidisciplinary and interfaculty research
  • Encouragement and Opportunity for real life / industrial research projects
