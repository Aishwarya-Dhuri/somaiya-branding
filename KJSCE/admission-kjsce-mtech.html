How to become a Somaiya student

K J Somaiya College of Engineering offers M Tech Programme in Computer Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Information Technology with Specialization in Information Security, Mechanical Engineering with specialization in CAD,CAM & Robotics and interdisciplinary program in Energy Engineering which is managed by Mechanical Engineering Department.

Admission to M Tech Programme will be offered on the basis of Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) or Somaiya Post Graduate Entrance Examination in Engineering (SPG-E 2020)

Scholarships

The Management of the college has decided to offer the scholarship to GATE Qualified candidates. This Scholarship is equivalent to 50% of the tuition fee of the current academic year. It will be provided to TOP TWO GATE Qualified of each program. It will be continued to such candidates in second year as per the college policy.

  • The Candidate should be an Indian National
  • Passed Bachelor Degree in the relevant field of Engineering and Technology with at least 50% marks (at least 45% marks in case of persons with disability) from any of the following categories of institutions
    1. All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) approved institutions
    2. University Grants Commission (UGC) approved Universities
    3. Central Government approved institutions
    4. State Government approved institutions
  • Obtained non zero positive score in Graduates Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE 2020) conducted by Indian Institute of Technology
    OR
    Obtained Qualified Score in Graduates Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE 2019 or 2018) conducted by Indian Institute of Technology
    OR
    Obtained Score in Somaiya Post Graduate Entrance Examination in Engineering (SPG-E 2020) conducted by K J Somaiya College of Engineering, A Constituent College of Somaiya Vidyavihar University
    OR
    For sponsored candidates, minimum of two years of full time work experience in a registered firm/ company/industry/ educational and/ or research institute/ any Government Department or Government Autonomous Organization in the relevant field in which admission is being sought.

Step1

Step 1

Eligible candidates interested for admission are required to submit the online application form available on College Admission website https://admissions.somaiya.edu/   

Step2

Step 2

There will be two phases in admission process

  • Phase 1 on the basis of Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) -2020 (Positive Score/ Qualified Score) OR GATE – 2019 / 2018 - (Only Qualified Score).
  • ​Phase 2 on the basis of Somaiya Post Graduate Entrance Examination in Engineering (SPG-E 2020).
Step3

Step 3

Application Form Fee is Rs. 2,000/- (non-refundable).

Step4

Step 4

Candidates appearing for SPG-E 2020 will have to pay additional Rs. 2,000/- towards examination fees (non-refundable)

Step5

Step 5

Candidates can apply for all the phases in the same application form by selecting appropriate options provided that the candidate is eligible for the respective phase.

Step6

Step 6

Admission will be offered based on the merit, preference, and availability of seats taken together. The candidate will be communicated via email about his/her offered seat or waitlisted number.

About Somaiya Post Graduate Entrance Examination in Engineering (SPG-E)

Somaiya Post Graduate Entrance Examination in Engineering (SPG-E 2020) is the entrance Examination for admission to First Year M. Tech. Engineering programmes at KJSCE for the year 2020-21. Admission will be given to aspiring candidates based on the merit of SPG-E 2020 or GATE Score.

Mode of Examination

SPG-E 2020 will be conducted online/offline and will have objective type / multiple choice questions (MCQs).

Choice of Medium of Question Papers

The medium of Question Paper will be English

Eligibility for SPG-E

Qualifying Degree Qualifying Degree/Examination Description of Eligible Candidates Year of Qualification not later than
BE / B Tech Bachelor’s degree holders in Engineering / Technology (4 years after 10+2 or 3 years after Diploma in Engineering / Technology) Currently in the final year* or already completed 2020

Note:  *Subject to fulfillment of the eligibility Criteria for M Tech Program
