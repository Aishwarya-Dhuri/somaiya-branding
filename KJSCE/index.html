About K J Somaiya College of Engineering

K J Somaiya College of Engineering is a constituent college of the prestigious Somaiya Vidyavihar University. It was established in 1983, on the 50 acres lush green campus of Somaiya Vidyavihar in a prominent suburb of Mumbai.

K J Somaiya College of Engineering is a student-focused college committed to excellent teaching & research, as well as access and affordability.The college has been awarded 'A' grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) in the year 2017, valid for 5 years. With recommendations from the University Grant Commission (UGC) and the Government of Maharashtra the college has received Autonomous status since 2014.

Orion Racing India

Orion Racing India the official formula student team of K.J Somaiya College of Engineering was born. Budding engineering students from various streams develop, design and manufacture a formula style car to take part in National and International Design Competitions in Germany, Italy and Hungary. Orion Racing India is the first Formula Student team in India to successfully build an Aerospace grade Carbon Fibre Monocoque chassis for their latest combustion prototype Bellatrix.
