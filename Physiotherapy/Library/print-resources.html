Books

Textbooksand reference books are the primary source of information. Physiotherapy college library has a well equipped with various national and international titles on different topics related to Physiotherapy.

Books were stacked, subject wise in the cupboards. Open Access facility is available in the library so that students can able to handle different books on the same topic.

Journals

Journals are the updated, evidence based source of information which supplement research activities.

It is possible to access latest information as is required regularly by students & faculty members.

The library has good collection of national and international journals on physiotherapy subjects. Back volumes of Journals have been neatly bound and are stacked in the cupboard.

Photocopying-Printing-Scanning facility

Facilities like Photocopying, Printing, Scanning are provided to the students. Students can avail photocopying and printing facility on payment of prescribed fees, within the premises of the library.

Please remember that photocopying a complete document is violation of copyright act.
