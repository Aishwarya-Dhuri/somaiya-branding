Experiential Learning

Other than clinical exposure in Somaiya hospital, it is important for students to experience and understand various other factors (eg:vocation) required for complete rehabilitation of a patient. It is also essential for them to visualize and learn about special groups of patients ( eg: burns, cancer) with different rehabilitation needs. Hence the college arranges visits to specific National rehabilitation centers which helps to widen their outlook.

Vocational Rehabilitation Center, Chunabhatti

This institute is now named as "National Career Service Center for differently abled". This institute works on vocational rehabilitation of differently abled person so as to achieve financial Independence. Students get to learn about different means of vocational rehabilitation such as tailoring, mechanics, electronics etc.

Central Labour Institute, Sion

This institute works in the area of industrial health problems and environmental engineering. The Factories Act, 1948 was designed and modeled by CLI. Physiotherapy students are benefited by getting insights of Industrial Therapy.

All India Institute of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation

This institute deals with comprehensive rehabilitation of differently abled persons. Students get to learn about rehabilitation team and role of each team members.

Tata Memorial Hospital, Parel

A lecture is organised on role of PT in Oncology. Information is provided with respect to breast class as a preventive action. Students get to see different cancer patients and their line of management.

National Burns Center, Airoli

A lecture is organised about pathophysiology of burns, Medical and surgical management with special emphasis on complications and physio-therapeutic management. Physiotherapy students get exposure to various equipment used for splinting burn contracture, procedure of skin testing.
