Laboratories

Following laboratories are available for undergraduate as well as postgraduate teaching:

  • Labs like kinesiotherapy, electrotherapy and electrodiagnosis are available with cubicles to give hands on training to the students.
  • A central research laboratory with the latest of equipment is available for research, patient care and postgraduate teaching. It includes EMG- NCV machine , Balance Master, Isokinetic system to name a few.
  • A separate yoga lab is available to teach patients and students yoga.
  • A fitness lab is made which has treadmills, cross trainers, hydropneumatic rowing machines and machines for circuit training and strengthening.

Canteen

The College also has a canteen for students, staff as well as patients.

As is the policy of Somaiya group of institutions, only vegetarian food is allowed on campus and thus the canteen too serves pure vegetarian food.

The food is prepared by taking all the steps to maintain cleanliness and hygiene while cooking.

Along with this the food is served for the health conscious , and so is cooked with the best of ingredients.

The canteen is fully equipped to seat at least 85 people at a time.

WiFi Connectivity

K.J.Somaiya College of Physiotherapy boasts a campus which looks old school and has an old world charm to it. At the same time it has modern facilities for students to enhance learning and facilitate an environment to make learning fun and interesting.

The campus has WiFi connectivity. There is WiFi available in all the classrooms, library, central research lab and common room.

It is also available in the administration offices to make communication and work via email and computers effective and fast.

Due to available of internet, the students can access various portals on the internet anywhere on campus.

Conference Room

The college has a spacious conference room available for teaching, workshops as well as meetings.

It has broad band as.well as WiFi facility available. It has the best of audio visual aids installed for effective lectures or meetings conducted there.

The conference room is well lit and is air conditioned for a comfortable as well as quiet area for effective meetings and lectures for a group of 50 persons at a time.

Student's Common Room

It is located in the heart of the college. There is a separate common room for boys as well as girls.

It is a well ventilated and lit. There are lockers available for students and seating. The entrance of the common room is done up beautifully to welcome the students.

There is WiFi available for students in the common room so that they can use their laptops to browse or study together.

Hostel

The Physiotherapy College has shared Hostel facilities with the K.J.Somaiya Medical College at the Ayurvihar Campus as well at the Somaiya Vidyavihar campus.

Both the hostels have spacious rooms with messes with clean and healthy food.

The campus is purely vegetarian as is the Somaiya culture. There is a facility for 10 girls and 5 boy students every year for physiotherapy.

Van Facilities

K J Somaiya College of Physiotherapy has started van service from August, 2016. This van service is for easy commute of patients needing regular physical therapy at nominal costs.

This facility has also been extended to the students of physiotherapy as well to commute between Sion station & College.

Auditorium

The college shares the auditorium with the facility available on the Ayurvihar Campus at the 3rd floor of the Medical College and an equally good auditorium at the Engineering college.

Various events like college festivals and guest lectures and workshops are regularly held there.

Medical Facility

The students of the college get all the medical facilities available at the K.J.Somaiya Hospital and Research Centre.

There is medical care also available in case of emergency at the casualty in the Hospital. Utmost and the best medical care is given to the students for their medical needs.
