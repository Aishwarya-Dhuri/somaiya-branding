Local Management Committee

The Local Management Committee is a task force set up as per the guidelines of Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS). It comprises members from the Management, Members from Teaching and Non - Teaching staff and experts in different fields nominated by the Management. The Committee meets twice a year to discuss various issues related to Undergraduate and Postgraduate Teaching and to ensure smooth functioning and development of the College.

The Somaiya College of Physiotherapy has formed a Local Managing Committee comprising renowned experts from various fields. The meeting of members of the Local Managing committee is convened twice a year and aims at monitoring the progress of the institute by periodically reviewing the activities of the institution. Issues regarding admissions, finance,budget, academics, grievances, student and teachers related matter are addressed. Action is taken in a systematic manner.
