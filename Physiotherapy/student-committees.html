Student Committees

Student Council

Comprises of students and faculty as per MUHS norms. This committee is elected annually by the the students. The Committee is responsible in organising, hosting, and encouraging student participation and interaction in various curricular and co-curricular student activities within the Institute, inter Institutes and University level. Student Council is a platform which is created by the students, for the students and of the students where they showcase their talents.

Magazine Committee

The College Magazine team works to bring out the annual official e- magazine “GLINT” - Illuminating Ideas. Comprises of students and faculty which unfolds the hidden writers, artists, journalists, entrepreneurs and alumni contributions.
