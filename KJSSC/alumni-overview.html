Alumni Overview

With a strong alumni base spread across the globe, it is our constant endeavour to connect & collaborate with our alumni.

With many of our alumni becoming researchers, industry leaders and teachers we take pride in them and celebrate their success We involve our alumni into many activities we do at KJSSC. From inviting alumni as guest speakers to share their knowledge & experience with students, as jury members for various events, as Chief Guest & Keynote speakers for Conferences, to mentor our students, etc. We also focus on alumni to alumni connect which helps younger alumni professionally.
