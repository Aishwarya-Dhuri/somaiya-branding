Library@KJSSC

The college library was setup in 1971. Today it has more than 69,000 books with addition of e-resources as well. The growth of the library collections has been in tune with the growing needs of institution. ...
The Objective of the Library is:
To acquire, develop, maintain and provide a qualitative and quantitative collection of books, periodicals and other instructional material to support the academic program and educational objectives of the college. To include in collection development many fields of knowledge as well as of career and character building. To provide quality services to faculty and students to support for achieving their curricular as well as co-curricular goals, with the help of technology, but not as a substitute for personal service. To inculcate reading habit among students and to encourage collaborative efforts among library staff and students

Services

Details of specialized services provided by the library

  • Inter-library loan facility within the Somaiya Campus
  • Reference Service for research projects
  • Free Internet access to students and staff
  • Reprographic Services
  • Overnight book issue facility during exam
  • Book Bank facility for economically backward students
  • Integrated searching of all resources available in the campus on OPAC and also on web base OPAC
  • Remote Access Link provided to Online subscribed Journals : N-List and EBSCOHOST Discovery Services, JGate, Springer Link, Taylor and Francis link, and Web of Science. The username and password forEbsco is the mail id and password provided by the college. Only For N-List contact the librarian for username and password.
