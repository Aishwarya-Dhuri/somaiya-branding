Convocation

Convocation is an exciting moment to mark the achievements of our students. All students who successfully complete a programme are eligible to participate in this prestigious event.

The Examination Committee sends the data of the final result to the Mumbai University for the degree certificates. The degree distribution ceremony is conducted by the college after the University’s degree distribution ceremony.

Oath

“We, the graduates and Post-graduates of K J Somaiya College of Science and Commerce,

Vidyavihar, Mumbai, hereby pledge that we will be scrupulously honest in all our activities and act with integrity at all times to uphold the honour and dignity of our profession and of our institute;

That we will be environmentally responsible and will actively protect and promote the wellbeing of our environment.

That we will uphold and promote the unity and secular ideals of our country. That, we will utilize our knowledge in the service of our country in its march towards a just, inclusive and sustainable society“
