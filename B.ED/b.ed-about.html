About Us

K J Somaiya College of Education (KJSCED),
A constituent college of Somaiya Vidyavihar University.

Offers B. Ed, M. Ed. Programs approved by the National Council of Teacher Education (NCTE) and is also Ph.D Centre.

Additionally, it offers a few Diploma & Certificate Programs such as Post Graduate Diploma in Educational Management and Research Methodology, School Counselling Education, Experiential learning for Environmental Education, Educational Technology, Innovative Assessment in Education and many more under Somaiya Vidyavihar University.

The faculty members are highly qualified and experienced, equipped with expertise in various academic disciplines such as Languages & Literature, Natural, Biological & Applied Sciences, Social Sciences & Humanities and Research as well as Technological skills. An excellent infrastructure with a well equipped Computer, Science and Language Lab, Sports as well as medical facilities and variety of educational resources aid in providing quality education.

The college experiments with innovative methodology of teaching and has collaborated with reputed schools of Mumbai and around to provide adequate pre-service training to Student Teachers. Holistic development is sought through choice based innovative assessment approaches, Co-curricular activities and Community Work.
