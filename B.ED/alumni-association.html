About Alumni Association

Alumni association of K J Somaiya College of Education is one of the biggest benefactor of the institution that contribute towards the developmental activities of the institution

The Alumni of the institution are regularly called for their active role in voluntary programs like mentoring students in their areas of expertise – like Demonstration of their lessons, orienting them about the school scenarios, needs of the industry and also play a significant role in making the students aware about the importance of acquiring skills essential for sustaining and aspiring in their profession.

Alumni is also a huge talent pool whose guidance can be beneficial to many students and other fellow-alumni in their respective areas of study.

The students of the institution are well groomed in applying the knowledge and values in their daily lives. The value of giving back to society is so well anchored in them that even after leaving the college the love for their alma mater calls them to give back the knowledge and values that they acquired from their teachers to the current batch of students; as a sign of their gratitude and affinity towards the institution.

The institution has a strong alumni network. The attachment that the alumni have towards their alma mater is dependent on the efforts that the institution takes to make their days memorable as students.
