What is duration of the B.Ed program?
Duration of B. Ed. program is 4 semesters (2 years)
How can I get admission for a B.Ed course?
Candidates seeking admission to B.Ed program have to clear either Somaiya B.Ed Common Entrance Test or MHA-CET.
What are the job prospects after B.Ed?
Candidates with B. Ed. degree are eligible to become Teachers in Elementary, Primary, SecondarySchools of different Boards and Junior Colleges as well as become Supervisors, Curriculum Designers and Developers, Work in NGOs related to education
When does admissions begin for the B.Ed Course in Mumbai ?
In the month of March-April
Can I pursue B. Ed. after B. Tech./BBA/BAF/BMS/BMM/BBI?
Yes, you can do B.Ed.with graduation of these disciplines.
What are the eligibility requirements for B.Ed?
Graduation (B.Sc, BA, B. Com, B. E, B. B. A, B. M. M, B. A. F, B. M. S, B. B. I. or allied graduation) or Post graduation (M.Sc, M. A., M. Com. etc)
Is there any in-house quota for Somaiya students?
No, there is no in-house quota for Somaiya students.
What subjects can we teach after completing a B.Ed?
Same as or related to your core specialization at graduation/post-graduation level.

What is duration of the M.Ed program?
Duration of M. Ed. program is 4 semesters (2 years)

Candidates seeking admission to M.Ed program have to clear either Somaiya M.Ed Common Entrance Test or MAH-CET.
What are the job prospects after M.Ed?
Candidates with M. Ed. degree are eligible to become Teacher Educators as well as become Principals, Supervisors, Curriculum Designers and Developers, Work in NGOs related to Education and Edupreneurs.
When does admissions begin for the M.Ed Course in Mumbai ?
In the month of March-April
Can I do M.Ed without B.Ed/BA ( Edu) ?
No. B.Ed or BA ( Education) is mandatory for seeking admission in M.Ed
What are the eligibility requirements for M.Ed?
B.Ed/B.A (Education) with 50%

No, there is no in-house quota for Somaiya students.

What makes this M.Ed program unique?
Compulsory Dissertation, theory courses, internship, academic writing, research seminars, research paper presentations/ publications.
Do I need post graduation like MSc, MA , MCom etc for seeking admission in M.Ed?
No . B.Ed/BA (Education) is the minimum requirement .
My undergraduate degree is not in the field of education, can I still apply?
No, the admission requirement for the M.Ed course is Bachelor of Education. B.Ed/B A (Education ) from UGC approved university.
What is duration of the PGDEM program?
Duration of PGDEM program is of 2 semesters. (1 year)
How can I get admission for a PGDEM course?
You can directly apply for provisional admission.
What are the job prospects after PGDEM?
Candidates with PGDEM degree are eligible to become Supervisors and Coordinators in schools and colleges. This course enhances their professional expertise.
When does admissions begin for the PGDEM Course in Mumbai ?
In the month of July
Can I pursue PGDEM with a full time job
Yes, you can do PGDEM along with full time job as this course is part time
What are the eligibility requirements for PGDEM?
Graduation (B.Sc, BA, BCom, B. E, BBA, BMM, BAF, BMS, BBI. or allied graduation) or Post graduation (M.Sc, M. A., M. Com. etc) or B.Ed.
Is there any in-house quota for Somaiya students?
No, there is no in-house quota for Somaiya students.
What is the nature of PGDEM course?
It consists of courses related to theories of educational management, organizational behaviour, human resource management, entrepreneurship and institutional planning
