Promoting Research

Creating an research encouraging space

Somaiya Institute for Research and Consultancy (SIRAC) functions as a think tank as well as task force to nucleate research ideas, promote, nurture and facilitate collaborative & interdisciplinary research projects cutting across disciplines and Higher Education Institutes of Somaiya Vidyavihar and Ayurvihar. SIRAC encourages a research ecosystem through knowledge sharing, capacity building and research promotion.

The hallmark of excellence for any University/College/Institute is the research that takes place under its leadership. Somaiya Vidyavihar delivers an individualistic as well as a collaborative approach towards both basic and application-oriented research in disciplines of Management, Science, Technology, Education, Health Sciences, Religion, Culture, and Humanities. The potential to carry out significant multi and inter-disciplinary research projects exists within its portals, which continues to be harnessed for better research outcomes. Research Promotions intends to promote inter institutional research, connect faculty and students & collate research information across Somaiya.
