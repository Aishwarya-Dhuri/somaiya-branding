The Collection

The approximate yearly budget varies from Rs. 6,00,000 to Rs. 7,00,000 including periodicals and journals.

Nearly 2000 to 2500 books are added every year.

Yearly 50 periodicals are subscribed, out of which four are foreign publications. Many journals are bound after completion of the volume.

Besides books and journals it has a good collection of CD-ROMs and maps and some audio and video cassettes. Past question papers and syllabi are available for reference. Library helps to get Xerox copies of syllabi to the students gone for further studies in foreign countries.

Library also maintains special collection on Career Guidance, Gandhian Literature, material for competitive examinations and CA studies, collection of Biographies. It also has some rare books, which are not available in market and now beyond copyright laws.
