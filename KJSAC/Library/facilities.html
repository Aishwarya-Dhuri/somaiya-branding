Facilities

The various services provided by library reflect our objectives. They are as below.

Library orientation and Library guide

Orientation is provided to newly admitted students.

Manual and computerised catalogue

Students are guided personally for using these catalogues.

Circulation of Library materials for current reading

The textbooks, question papers, newspapers, current periodicals, general books are provided.

Home lending of books

Textbooks of all subjects for seven days home lending.

Current Issue

At the Current Reading Counter & Reference Counter, the textbooks, general books, question papers are provided for current reading only, against the Identity Card.

Periodicals / newspapers

Current periodicals from Current Reading Counter, back issues from Reference Counter, newspapers from Home Lending Counter can be referred within library.

Reference Service

Books other than textbooks are issued and reference sources are available for current reading. We personally help students to select books according to their interest.

Question papers of previous year

Files are kept at Current Reading Counter and are available to refer within the library.

Syllabus

To be referred within library.

Book-Bank

Textbooks are provided for one academic year against the deposit same as the cost of books, which is fully refundable.

Reading Room

418 chairs are provided at present and likely to be increased.

CD-ROMs viewing on multimedia computers

The shows are arranged for not more than eight students at a time. Students can also watch them individually or under the guidance of teachers.

Referral service

We guide students to another library if the books of their interest are not available in our library.

Display of newly arrived books

These are displayed in two cupboards and are available there during the complete academic year. These books are issued.

Photocopy Facility

We provide photocopy facility to staff and students.

Overnight book reading facility

We provide Overnight Book Reading Facility. Whenever required by student the book can be issued for overnight only from Current Reading Counter when textbooks will not available on Home Issue Counter.

Vocational guidance

A file of various careers available in Mumbai or near Mumbai is maintained in the library. Various information and advertisements are put up on the notice board. We have various books and periodicals for career guidance and competitive examinations.

Listing of books

According to Subject, Author through computer program. But any kinds of lists are prepared for different purposes according to requirement. Students can use library computers for preparing lists of their interest.

Printing

Both are available for strictly educational purpose to staff under guidance from library staff.

Books exhibitions and displays

It is a regular feature of library to exhibit books on various themes from time to time throughout the year. The subject-wise exhibitions in collaboration with other departments are also arranged.

Internet

Access to the internet is provided free of charge to all including students and staff strictly for educational purposes only.

Prepare and printing bio-data

Students can use computers to prepare bio-data and various applications.

Computer practice

Whenever the computers in second network are free, the students are permitted to practice programming on them.
