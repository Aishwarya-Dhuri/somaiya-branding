Library@Ayurvihar

A posh library, facilitated with 4000 textbooks of latest literature in medical & nursing sciences enriches students' leaning activity. The literature as prescribed in the University syllabus, and recommended by the Indian Nursing council makes a reference library. In addition, foreign journals, Indian Nursing Journals, and Magazines such as Health action, Herald of Health, Social Welfare and News Papers are also stacked for leisure time reading to enhance students' general knowledge. The E-learning Library is facilitated to students for the purpose literature review, study and research work. A well qualified full time librarian is positioned to maximize the use of library resources. Motivating students to use the library for self guided study, is an important role executed by a librarian.

  • Marathi Bhasha Divas

    Our college celebrated Marathi Bhasha Diwas on 27th Feb 2020 which is the birth anniversary of well known Marathi writer and poet Shri V. Va. Shirwadkar. Students participated in the different competitions like speeches, poems, skits, essay writing and posters etc to cultivate the importance of Marathi Language . The various information resources available in Marathi like books, magazines is displayed by library in display area.
