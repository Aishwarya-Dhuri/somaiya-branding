Books Circulation

Policy for circulation

Library is open all days, except Sunday and institute notified holidays. Students, faculty and staff can borrow library materials from the library between

Issue & return

10.30am to 6.00pm (Monday to Saturday)

Reading

8.30am to 6.00pm (Monday to Saturday)

Carrying identity card and library card is mandatory to all who enter the library. Only active (current) students, faculty and staff can issue books from library.

Individuals without identity and library card (external students, visiting faculty, alumni and guests) should contact librarian to complete their procedure for issue of books.

Reference Services

  • Current awareness service: Every year, a list of additions to the library collection is displayed on the notice board and copy send through social sites (whats app).
  • Library provides assistance in using databases to all researchers.
  • Reprography: A photocopy counter is available on the 6th floor of the college building. Library allows photocopies as well as printouts of library resources to faculty members.
  • Inter Library Loan: Global Common Circulation (GCC) is adopted for the campus libraries by which any student or faculty member belonging to any of SVV institution can avail the KJSCON library facility. KJSCON students can also avail library facility of other SVV institutes.
  • Information Deployment and Notification: User orientation and Information Literacy Drive
  • Reminders: Gentle Reminders email is send by the bookworm software to the users before one days of due dated, as well as overdue reminder email also send after due date.
  • Intimation Reminder email is sent to users if any book issued by them has a reservation.
  • Intimation email regarding availability of a reserved book is sent to the user who has reserved that book.
