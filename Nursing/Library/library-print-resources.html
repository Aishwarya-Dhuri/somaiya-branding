Books

Nursing college library has a well equipped with various national and international titles on different topics related to nursing. Library has a good collection of books pertaining to Nursing, fiction, motivational subjects.

Books were stacked, subject wise in the cupboards. Open Access facility is available in the library so that students can able to handle different books on the same topic.
